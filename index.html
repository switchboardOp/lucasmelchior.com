Krampus Ride

The Annual Krampus Charity Bike Ride benifits kids in need durring the holiday season. 100% of the money donated goes purchasing toys at local nonprofit toy shop Kiddywampus, as well as books at Wild Rumpus. Each year we deliver them by bike to the People Serving People Homeless Shelter in Downtown Minneapolis. I've been a part of Krampus for 5+ years.

You can find more information about the ride here.
